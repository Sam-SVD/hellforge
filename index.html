HELLFORGE 8

A standalone retro 2.5D demon-shooter.

Move with WASD, aim with mouse or ←→, shoot with Click/Space, hold Right Click/Q for ADS.

Weapons: 1 Pistol, 2 Shotgun, 3 Rifle. Press Shift to sprint, M for map, P to pause, O to toggle music.

Objective: clear the monsters, then reach the glowing exit tile. Very loud original retro-rock/metal music starts after you click.

Click or press Enter to start.
